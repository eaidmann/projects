Smart Study System has become a new trend in the past few years and is considered as one of the key processes in the academic field.

The process of register the course and appointment is a frequent and important activity in any university .

A good appointment scheduling system encourages student and lectures satisfaction, and as such is an important component of study field.

Smart Study System is mainly designed for lecturers and student who wants to enrolled and organize the course systematically.
